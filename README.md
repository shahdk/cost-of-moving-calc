# Cost of Moving Calculator

**Know exactly what your move will cost — before you make it.**

The Cost of Moving Calculator is a free, interactive tool that compares your total tax burden and cost of living across US cities. Enter your financial details once, and instantly see how relocating would impact your bottom line — taxes, healthcare, food, childcare, gas, and more.

## Why Use This Calculator?

Moving to a new state affects far more than just your rent or mortgage. State income taxes, local taxes, property taxes, sales taxes, and everyday living costs vary dramatically across the country. A $150K salary in San Francisco has a very different take-home reality than $150K in Austin or Nashville.

This calculator gives you the **complete financial picture** — not just one piece of it.

---

## What It Compares

### Tax Burden
- **State income tax** — progressive brackets, flat rates, or no tax at all
- **Local income tax** — city and county-level taxes (e.g., NYC, Philadelphia, Columbus)
- **Property tax** — county-level rates with homestead exemptions, calculated from your home size and local median prices
- **Capital gains tax** — state-specific rates on long-term investment gains
- **Sales tax** — combined state + local rates applied to estimated annual spending
- **Estate & inheritance tax** — informational exposure by state

### Tax Benefits & Adjustments
- **529 education savings** — state deductions and credits for college savings plans
- **Child tax credits** — state-level credits per dependent child
- **HSA impact** — additional state taxes in non-conforming states (CA, NJ)
- **Retirement savings** — 401(k) and IRA deduction differences by state

### Cost of Living
- **Healthcare** — annual premiums and out-of-pocket costs by metro area
- **Food** — monthly grocery and food expenses by city
- **Childcare** — per-child annual costs (up to 3 children)
- **Gas** — based on local fuel prices and estimated annual mileage

### The Bottom Line
Everything rolls up into a single **Total Annual Cost** number for each city — taxes plus cost of living combined — so you can see the real dollar difference a move would make.

## How It Works

A guided, step-by-step wizard walks you through entering your information:

1. **Locations** — Where you live now and where you're considering (up to 5 destinations)
2. **Filing & Housing** — Tax filing status and home size
3. **Income** — Salary, spouse's salary, and whether it would change at your destination
4. **Additional Income** — Capital gains, rental income, and other sources
5. **Dependents** — Number of dependent children
6. **Retirement & Savings** — 401(k), IRA, HSA, and 529 contributions

Results update live as you fill in each section, so you can see estimates immediately — even before completing every field.

## Results & Visualization

### Interactive Map View
An interactive US map shows your current city connected to each destination with color-coded indicators:
- **Green** — destination is cheaper overall
- **Red** — destination is more expensive

Click any city marker to see a detailed cost breakdown right on the map.

### Table View
A side-by-side comparison table with every tax category and cost of living line item. The lowest-cost option in each row is highlighted in green, making it easy to spot where each city wins or loses.

Key metrics at a glance:
- Net annual tax burden with effective tax rate
- Total annual cost of living
- **Dollar and percentage change** vs. your current location

### Export
Export your full comparison as a PNG image — useful for sharing with a partner, financial advisor, or just saving for later.

## Coverage

## Data Sources

All calculations are built on publicly available data from authoritative sources:

- **Tax rates**: State revenue departments, Tax Foundation, Bloomberg Tax
- **Property taxes**: County assessor offices, state property tax databases
- **Home values**: Zillow Home Value Index, Redfin, Realtor.com
- **Healthcare costs**: Kaiser Family Foundation, BLS Consumer Expenditure Survey
- **Food costs**: USDA Food Plans, MIT Living Wage Calculator
- **Childcare costs**: Child Care Aware of America, Economic Policy Institute
- **Gas prices**: AAA, GasBuddy, U.S. Energy Information Administration
